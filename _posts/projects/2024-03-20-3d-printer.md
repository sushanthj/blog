# Building a 3D Printer

A journey of building and customizing a 3D printer from scratch.

## Components Used

- Ender 3 Pro frame
- SKR Mini E3 V2 board
- BLTouch sensor
- Direct drive extruder
- Glass bed

## Modifications

- Added auto-leveling
- Upgraded to direct drive
- Installed silent drivers
- Added LED lighting
- Custom firmware

## First Prints

- Calibration cube
- Benchy boat
- Temperature tower
- Retraction test
- Custom designs
