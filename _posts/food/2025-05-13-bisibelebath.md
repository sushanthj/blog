## Ingredients 

Bass
- **Toor Dal** : 1/2 cup
- **Rice** : 1/2 cup
- **Red Onion(optional)** : 1/4 American Onion or 1/2 Indian Onion
- **Beans** : 15
- **Peas** : 1/4 cup
- **Baby Carrots** : 5
- **Tomato** : 2 (Roma)
- **Peanuts** : 1/2 cup
- **Cashew** : 1/4 cup
- **Grated Coconut** : 1/4 cup

Treble
- **Mustard** : 1 teaspoon
- **Tamarind Concentrate** : 1.5 teaspoon
- **Bisibelebath Powder** : 3 Tablespoon
- **Turmeric** : 1/5 teaspoon
- **Salt** : 1.5 teaspoon


## Instructions
1. Cut one tomato, 5 baby carrots and quarter onion in a diced fashion and 1 or 2 Tomato 
2. Cut approx 3 Lego sized pieces of tamarind and soak in 1 cup of water
3. Wash and Soak 1/2 cup of rice and 1/2 cup of toor dal for 20 mins
4. In cooker/instant pot in saute mode, take 1TBSP of oil and heat. Add 1 tsp of mustard and  some peanuts and cashew
5.  After cashew gets a little charred, add the vegetables we cut above (tomato, carrot, onion) and let it fry for a minute
6. After a minute add beans and peas and 1/1.5 tsp turmeric and 1.5 tsp salt
7. Pour the tamarind stock (which is basically 1 cup of water) and add 3 cups of water (totally 4 cups)
8. Mix a bit and add 3 Tbsp of bisibelebath powder and 1/4 cup of grated coconut
9. Finally close lid and High pressure cook for 2 minutes. Let it release pressure naturally
