## Ingredients
- **Baby Spinach** : 4oz
- **Tomato** : 2
- **Dal** : 1 cup (50% toor dal, 30% masoor dal, 20% urad dal)
- **Garlic** : 3 cloves
- **Cumin** : 1 teaspoon
- **Mustard** : 1 teaspoon
- **Urad Dal** : 1 teaspoon
- **Curry Leaves** : 4
- **Green Chilly** : 2
- **Dried Red Chilly** : 2

## Instructions


1. Add 2 tbsp ghee and 1 tbsp oil

Keep spices ready:
- 3 cloves of garlic
- 1 TSP cumin
- 1 TSP mustard
- 1 tbsp urad dal
- 4 curry leaves
- 2 guntur chilly
- 2 green chilly

Once oil is ready add the above mix and let it cook. Then switch off instant pot.

Now add:
1. 1 cup dal washed (50% toor dal, 30% masoor dal, 20% urad dal)
2. 4oz palak
3. 2 cup water
4. 2 tomato
5. 1/2 tbsp tamarind
6. 1.5 TSP turmeric
7. 1.5 tbsp chilly powder
8. 1/2 tbsp salt

Then close lid and high pressure cook for 8 minutes
